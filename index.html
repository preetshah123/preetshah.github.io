  • LEGO NXT Robot

    Another project I am currently working on is programming a LEGO NXT Robot to perform different tasks using C++ and RobotC. The robot will be able to lift, store, transport, and unload different coloured objects to their designated spots.   • UWaterloo Formula Electric SAE team

    This design team is centered around building a fully electric Formula SAE racecar. I joined the team during my first term at the University of Waterloo because of my love for Formula 1. Currently, the team has taken the car apart fully so we can fix and improve the quality of certain parts, in preparation of the competition in mid June. The team is split into many different subdivisions as to improve efficiency and so I am part of the powertrain team. Currently, I am working on creating gaskets for the motor casing as to prevent any leakage. The SAE rules are very strict about leakage from the motor and this was something that the team failed on previously and we don't want to make the same mistake twice. Come check us out: Formula Electric UWaterloo
